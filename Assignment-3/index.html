"OUR STORY."

1930The very first SNICKERS Bar, allegedly named after a horse, was manufactured in Chicago and sold for five cents.

1967SNICKERS is called the Marathon Bar in the UK.

1968Fun Size SNICKERS is created, followed by an increase in satisfaction levels worldwide.

1984SNICKERS is an official sponsor of the 1984 LA Olympic Games. We take home gold in every non-sport event.

1989The SNICKERS Ice Cream Bar is introduced. We are forever changed.

2010The award-winning “You’re not you when you’re hungry” campaign is created and celebrated.

2013SNICKERS passes $1 Billion in sales in the US.
